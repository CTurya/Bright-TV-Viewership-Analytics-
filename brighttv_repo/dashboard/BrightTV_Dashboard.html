💡 Monday Low-Consumption: Content Recommendations
🎬 Reality & Drama Series
Africa Magic & Channel O already perform on Mondays. Commission Monday-exclusive reality/drama drops to give users a reason to tune in.
🏆 Sports Highlights
SuperSport Blitz performs well on high days. Run Monday match recaps & weekly sport previews to capture sports fans during the week dip.
🎵 Music Countdowns
Trace TV & Channel O are the top Monday channels. A weekly Monday music countdown show aligns directly with demonstrated preference.
👨‍👩‍👧 Family & Kids Block (18:00–20:00)
Cartoon Network & Boomerang are top Monday performers. A dedicated family viewing block at peak hour drives cross-household engagement.
🚀 Growth Initiatives to Expand User Base
📱 Female-Targeted Content Strategy
Females are only 12% of profiled users yet 9% of sessions. Invest in E! Entertainment, lifestyle & local drama to grow this segment.
🌍 Provincial Expansion
Gauteng-heavy base (38%). Targeted campaigns in KZN, Eastern Cape & Free State can unlock underpenetrated markets.
🎓 Youth Activation (18–24)
18–24s are the second largest group but have the lowest avg session time. Social-first short content and TikTok/Instagram tie-ins can increase stickiness.
📧 Re-engagement: 989 Inactive Profiles
~18% of profiles have never watched. A win-back campaign (free previews, personalised recommendations) can convert dormant sign-ups.
